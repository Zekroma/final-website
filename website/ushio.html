Ushio (Clannad After Story)

Video

Summary:
Ushio was a very young girl who was getting raised by an only father. The father and mother were happily in love and they end up pregnant. The mother goes through labor and gives birth successfully but she dies in the process. Heart tearing for the father he devotes all is love, hope, time, and everything with the child that she gave her life for. He raises the girl but one winter day she collapses and struggles to breathe. She ends up dying and knowing everything that has happened and seeing the devastation of the father's face is the most pain and sad thing to see, hear, even feel. All of the emotions going on during this moment and seeing the flashbacks will cause you to cry 100% of the time.
